About Me

UI/UX Designer!

Hello! I'm Tanaya Shrestha, a designer and perpetual learner delving into the world of UI/UX Design while pursuing my bachelor's degree in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT), currently in the 7th semester. Alongside my academic pursuits, I have been contributing to the online store 'Enhance Beauty' as a Graphic Designer (Digital Poster Designer). I love drawing, a passion that has been with me since childhood. I began creating digital arts and portraits during the pandemic, exploring and honing my artistic skills. Eager to enhance my design skills and continue my creative journey.
